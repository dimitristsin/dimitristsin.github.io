Rounds vs. Parallel Queries in Student-Teacher Witnessing

Slides

In this talk, we consider different classes of total search problems which are defined via the interactive framework of Student-Teacher Games, and we connect their separations with theories of Bounded Arithmetic, thus solving some open problems in the area.

Provability of the Circuit Size Hierarchy and Its Consequences

Slides

The circuit size hierarchy was proven by Shannon by an existential type argument. In this talk, we study how constructive a proof of the hierarchy could be, by considering its provability in different theories of Bounded Arithmetic.

Slides
