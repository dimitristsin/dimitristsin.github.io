About

∈

I am a PhD student at the University of Warwick, working at the intersection of computational complexity theory and algebraic structures. My research explores how mathematical logic and abstract algebra can provide new insights into fundamental questions about computation.

At Warwick, I'm part of the Theory and Foundations (FoCS) group and the Centre for Discrete Mathematics and its Applications (DIMAP), advised by Igor Carboni Oliveira and Christian Ikenmeyer.
